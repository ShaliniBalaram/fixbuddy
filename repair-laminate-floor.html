  • Replacement laminate planks
  • Circular saw or laminate cutter
  • Pry bar
  • Hammer
  • Wood glue
  • Utility knife
  • Safety glasses
  • Work gloves

Estimated Time: 1-2 hours

üìù Step-by-Step Instructions

  1. Assess the damage:
    • Identify damaged planks
    • Mark the area to be replaced
    • Ensure you have matching replacements
  2. Remove damaged planks:
    • Cut through center of damaged plank
    • Carefully pry out pieces
    • Remove any remaining debris
  3. Prepare replacement plank:
    • Cut to exact size if needed
    • Remove bottom groove on replacement
    • Test fit before final installation
  4. Install new plank:
    • Apply wood glue to edges
    • Slide into place at angle
    • Press down firmly
    • Clean excess glue immediately
  5. Finishing touches:
    • Place heavy weight on seams
    • Allow glue to dry completely
    • Clean area thoroughly
