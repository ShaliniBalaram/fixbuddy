Replace Door Weather Stripping

Stop drafts and save energy with proper weather stripping

üí° Energy Saving Tip:

Proper weather stripping can reduce energy costs by up to 15% by preventing air leaks around doors.

  • New weather stripping
  • Utility knife
  • Measuring tape
  • Screwdriver
  • Scissors
  • Clean rags
  • Adhesive cleaner (if needed)

Estimated Time: 1-2 hours

üìù Step-by-Step Instructions

  1. Remove old weather stripping:
    • Pull off old strips carefully
    • Remove any remaining adhesive
    • Clean the door frame thoroughly
  2. Measure for new weather stripping:
    • Measure each side of the door frame
    • Add extra length for corners
    • Purchase appropriate type and length
  3. Prepare the surface:
    • Clean with soap and water
    • Let dry completely
    • Remove any rust or loose paint
  4. Install new weather stripping:
    • Start at the top of the door
    • Press firmly into place
    • Cut corners at 45-degree angles
    • Continue down sides
  5. Test the seal:
    • Close door completely
    • Look for light gaps
    • Feel for drafts
    • Adjust if needed
