üìù Step-by-Step Instructions

  1. Safety first:
    • Turn off power at breaker
    • Remove outlet cover plate
    • Test with voltage tester
  2. Inspect the outlet:
    • Check for loose mounting screws
    • Look for damaged wires
    • Check box alignment
  3. Tighten mounting screws:
    • Don't overtighten
    • Use outlet shims if needed
    • Ensure outlet sits flush
  4. Check wiring connections:
    • Ensure wire screws are tight
    • Look for loose wire nuts
    • Check ground wire connection
  5. Complete the repair:
    • Replace cover plate
    • Turn power back on
    • Test outlet function
