Secure Loose Rugs to Prevent Slips

Learn how to properly secure area rugs and mats to prevent slipping hazards and maintain a safe living environment.

Safety Warning

Unsecured rugs are a major trip and fall hazard, especially for elderly people and children. Always ensure rugs are properly secured.
