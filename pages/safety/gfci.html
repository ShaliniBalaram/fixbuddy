Test and Reset GFCI Outlets

Learn how to properly test and maintain Ground Fault Circuit Interrupter (GFCI) outlets to ensure they're working correctly to protect against electrical shock.

Critical Safety Warning

GFCI outlets are crucial safety devices. Never bypass or disable them. If an outlet repeatedly trips, have it inspected by a licensed electrician.

Understanding GFCI Outlets

Purpose

  • Code compliance

Testing Steps

Step 1: Visual Inspection

  • Check for damage
  • Look for discoloration
  • Verify buttons
  • Check cover

Step 2: Button Test

  • Press TEST button
  • Verify trip
  • Press RESET
  • Check power

Step 3: Device Test

  • Use GFCI tester
  • Check response time
  • Verify protection
  • Test downstream

Step 4: Documentation

  • Record test date
  • Note results
  • Plan next test
  • Mark calendar
