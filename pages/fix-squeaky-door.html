Fix a Squeaky Door

A quick and easy solution to eliminate door squeaks

  • WD-40 or silicone-based lubricant
  • Clean cloth or paper towels
  • Screwdriver (if hinge pins need tightening)

Estimated Time: 10-15 minutes

üìù Step-by-Step Instructions

  1. Identify the source of the squeak by slowly opening and closing the door. The noise usually comes from the hinges.
  2. Clean the hinges with a dry cloth to remove any dust or debris.
  3. Apply lubricant to the hinge pins:
    • Spray WD-40 or apply silicone lubricant directly into the hinge
    • Place paper towels under the hinge to catch any drips
    • Open and close the door several times to work in the lubricant
  4. Wipe away any excess lubricant with a clean cloth.
  5. If squeaking persists, you may need to:
    • Tighten any loose hinge screws
    • Remove and clean the hinge pins
    • Replace worn hinges if necessary
