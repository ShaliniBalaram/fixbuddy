How to Fix Squeaky Door Hinges

Beginner Friendly 10-20 minutes

Squeaky door hinges can be annoying but are usually easy to fix. Learn how to silence those noisy hinges with these simple solutions.

Common Problems & Solutions

Basic Squeaky Hinge

Most squeaky hinges just need proper lubrication to function quietly.

Solution:

  1. Clean hinge thoroughly
  2. Apply lubricant
  3. Move door back and forth
  4. Wipe excess lubricant
  5. Test door movement

Rusty Hinges

Rust can cause squeaking and make movement difficult.

Solution:

  1. Remove rust with steel wool
  2. Clean debris thoroughly
  3. Apply rust inhibitor
  4. Lubricate hinge
  5. Test operation

Loose or Damaged Hinge

Sometimes the problem is more than just squeaking.

Solution:

  1. Check all screws
  2. Tighten loose screws
  3. Replace damaged screws
  4. Consider hinge replacement
  5. Adjust alignment

What You'll Need

Lubricant (WD-40 or similar)
Screwdriver
Cleaning cloth
Steel wool (if needed)
Replacement screws

Maintenance Tips

Lubricate hinges every 6 months to prevent squeaking

Clean hinges regularly to prevent dirt buildup

Check and tighten screws periodically

Address rust early to prevent permanent damage
