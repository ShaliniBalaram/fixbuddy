Tighten a Door Hinge

Learn how to fix loose door hinges and eliminate squeaking with this comprehensive guide.

Tools & Materials

  • Screwdriver (Phillips and flathead)
  • Wood shims
  • Replacement screws (if needed)
  • Wood filler or toothpicks
  • Wood glue
  • WD-40 or similar lubricant
  • Hammer
  • Safety glasses

Fix Steps

Step 1: Assess the Problem

  • Check all hinge screws
  • Look for loose or stripped screws
  • Test door movement
  • Listen for squeaking

Step 2: Tighten Loose Screws

  • Tighten all visible screws
  • Check for proper door alignment
  • Use longer screws if needed
  • Test door operation

Step 3: Fix Stripped Screw Holes

  • Remove loose screws
  • Insert wood filler or toothpicks with glue
  • Let dry completely
  • Re-drill pilot holes if necessary

Step 4: Lubricate and Test

  • Apply lubricant to hinge pins
  • Work door back and forth
  • Wipe excess lubricant
  • Test door operation

Common Problems

Squeaking Hinges

Solution: Clean and lubricate hinge pins

Door Sagging

Solution: Replace with longer screws or reinforce hinge mounting

Stripped Screw Holes

Solution: Fill holes with wood filler or toothpicks and wood glue

Maintenance Tips

  • Lubricate hinges every 6 months
  • Check screws quarterly
  • Clean hinges regularly
  • Avoid hanging items on doors
  • Address squeaks promptly
