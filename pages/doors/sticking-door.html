Fix a Sticking Door

Learn how to fix a door that sticks, rubs, or doesn't close properly.

Tools & Materials

  • Sandpaper (medium and fine grit)
  • Pencil
  • Utility knife
  • Wood plane or rasp
  • Level
  • Screwdriver
  • Wood sealer or paint
  • Safety glasses and dust mask

Fix Steps

Step 1: Identify the Problem

  • Open and close door to find sticking points
  • Mark problem areas with pencil
  • Check if door is level
  • Inspect hinges for looseness

Step 2: Check Hinges

  • Tighten all hinge screws
  • Replace any stripped screws
  • Check hinge alignment
  • Add shims if needed

Step 3: Sand or Plane

  • Remove door if necessary
  • Sand or plane marked areas
  • Work gradually and test fit
  • Keep edges even

Step 4: Finish and Rehang

  • Seal exposed wood
  • Paint or finish as needed
  • Rehang door carefully
  • Test operation

Common Problems

Seasonal Changes

Solution: Allow extra clearance for wood expansion in humid weather

Uneven Frame

Solution: Use shims to adjust door alignment in frame

Over-sanding

Solution: Work gradually and test fit frequently

Maintenance Tips

  • Keep hinges clean and lubricated
  • Monitor seasonal changes
  • Address sticking promptly
  • Maintain consistent humidity levels
  • Check door alignment regularly
