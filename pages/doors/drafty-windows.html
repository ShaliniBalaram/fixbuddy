Seal Drafty Windows

Learn how to eliminate cold drafts and improve your home's energy efficiency.

Tools & Materials

  • Weather stripping
  • Caulk and caulk gun
  • Utility knife
  • Scissors
  • Cleaning supplies
  • Measuring tape
  • Draft detector (candle or incense)
  • Safety glasses and gloves

Fix Steps

Step 1: Locate Drafts

  • Use draft detector around window edges
  • Mark draft locations
  • Check both inside and outside
  • Test window operation

Step 2: Clean and Prepare

  • Remove old caulk and weatherstripping
  • Clean surfaces thoroughly
  • Let area dry completely
  • Measure areas for materials

Step 3: Apply Weather Stripping

  • Cut strips to size
  • Apply to window frame
  • Ensure proper compression
  • Test window operation

Step 4: Caulk and Seal

  • Apply caulk to gaps
  • Smooth beads with finger or tool
  • Clean excess immediately
  • Allow to cure fully

Common Problems

Window Won't Close

Solution: Adjust weatherstripping thickness or position

Caulk Not Adhering

Solution: Ensure surface is clean and dry before application

Persistent Drafts

Solution: Consider using both weatherstripping and caulk

Maintenance Tips

  • Check seals seasonally
  • Clean window tracks regularly
  • Replace weatherstripping when compressed
  • Monitor for water leaks
  • Inspect caulk annually
