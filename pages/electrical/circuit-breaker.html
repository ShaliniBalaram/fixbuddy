How to Reset and Fix Circuit Breaker Issues

Beginner 5-15 minutes

Circuit breakers trip to protect your home from electrical hazards. Learn how to safely reset and troubleshoot common circuit breaker problems.

If breakers trip frequently or you smell burning, contact a licensed electrician immediately!

Common Problems & Solutions

Tripped Breaker

Circuit breaker has switched to the OFF position.

Solution:

  1. Locate breaker box
  2. Find tripped breaker (in middle position)
  3. Turn breaker fully OFF
  4. Then flip firmly to ON
  5. Verify power restored

Frequent Trips

Breaker trips repeatedly after resetting.

Solution:

  1. Unplug devices on circuit
  2. Reset breaker
  3. Reconnect one device at a time
  4. Identify overloaded items
  5. Redistribute loads if needed

Hot Breaker

Circuit breaker feels warm to touch.

Solution:

  1. Turn off breaker immediately
  2. Unplug devices on circuit
  3. Let breaker cool down
  4. Check for loose connections
  5. Contact electrician if persistent

What You'll Need

Flashlight
Circuit tester
Circuit directory
Electrician's number

Safety Tips

Never reset a breaker more than once

Keep breaker box area clear

Label all circuits clearly

Know main breaker location
