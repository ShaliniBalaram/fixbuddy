Fix a Loose Cabinet Handle

Learn how to tighten or replace loose cabinet handles and knobs quickly and easily.

Tools & Materials

  • Screwdriver (Phillips and flathead)
  • Replacement screws (if needed)
  • Wood filler (optional)
  • Drill and bits (if relocating)
  • Measuring tape
  • Pencil
  • Level
  • Safety glasses

Fix Steps

Step 1: Assess the Problem

  • Check if handle is loose
  • Inspect mounting holes
  • Look for stripped screws
  • Test cabinet door alignment

Step 2: Tighten Hardware

  • Remove handle completely
  • Clean mounting area
  • Check screw condition
  • Replace screws if needed

Step 3: Repair Stripped Holes

  • Fill old holes with wood filler
  • Allow to dry completely
  • Sand smooth if needed
  • Drill new pilot holes

Step 4: Reinstall Handle

  • Position handle carefully
  • Insert and tighten screws
  • Check alignment
  • Test operation

Common Problems

Misaligned Holes

Solution: Fill old holes and drill new ones in correct position

Stripped Screws

Solution: Use slightly larger screws or repair kit

Loose Door

Solution: Check and tighten door hinges first

Maintenance Tips

  • Check handles monthly
  • Clean handles regularly
  • Don't overtighten screws
  • Keep spare hardware
  • Address issues promptly
