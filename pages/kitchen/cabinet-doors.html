Realign Kitchen Cabinet Doors

Learn how to adjust and realign cabinet doors for proper closure and appearance.

Tools & Materials

  • Screwdriver
  • Level
  • Pencil
  • Measuring tape
  • Shims
  • Wood glue (if needed)
  • Replacement hinges (if needed)
  • Safety glasses

Fix Steps

Step 1: Assess Alignment

  • Check door gaps
  • Test door operation
  • Mark problem areas
  • Identify hinge type

Step 2: Adjust Hinges

  • Locate adjustment screws
  • Adjust up/down position
  • Adjust in/out position
  • Adjust left/right position

Step 3: Fine-Tune

  • Check door level
  • Adjust door spacing
  • Test door closure
  • Verify alignment

Step 4: Final Check

  • Tighten all screws
  • Test door movement
  • Check adjacent doors
  • Clean hinges

Common Problems

Uneven Gaps

Solution: Adjust hinge depth and angle screws

Door Rubbing

Solution: Adjust side-to-side positioning

Sagging Doors

Solution: Tighten mounting screws or replace hinges

Maintenance Tips

  • Check alignment monthly
  • Clean hinges regularly
  • Tighten screws periodically
  • Lubricate moving parts
  • Address issues promptly
