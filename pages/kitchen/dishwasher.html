Unclog a Dishwasher Drain

Learn how to fix a clogged dishwasher and prevent future drainage issues.

Tools & Materials

  • Pliers
  • Screwdriver
  • Vinegar
  • Baking soda
  • Drain snake or auger
  • Towels
  • Bucket
  • Cleaning brush

Fix Steps

Step 1: Initial Check

  • Remove and clean filter
  • Check drain hose
  • Inspect garbage disposal
  • Clear visible debris

Step 2: Natural Cleaning

  • Pour baking soda in drain
  • Add vinegar solution
  • Let sit for 15 minutes
  • Flush with hot water

Step 3: Deep Clean

  • Remove drain hose
  • Clean hose thoroughly
  • Snake the drain line
  • Check connections

Step 4: Test and Prevent

  • Reconnect all parts
  • Run empty cycle
  • Check for leaks
  • Install drain strainer

Common Problems

Standing Water

Solution: Check and clean filter, drain hose, and air gap

Bad Odors

Solution: Regular cleaning with vinegar and baking soda

Slow Draining

Solution: Clear garbage disposal and check drain line for partial clogs

Maintenance Tips

  • Scrape plates before loading
  • Clean filter weekly
  • Run hot water before starting
  • Use monthly cleaning tablets
  • Check spray arms regularly
