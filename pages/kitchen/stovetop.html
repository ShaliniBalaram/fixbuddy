Clean Burn Marks from Stovetop

Learn how to safely remove burn marks and restore your stovetop's appearance.

Tools & Materials

  • Baking soda
  • White vinegar
  • Dish soap
  • Non-abrasive scrubber
  • Soft cloths
  • Plastic scraper
  • Stovetop cleaner
  • Rubber gloves

Clean Steps

Step 1: Initial Cleaning

  • Let stovetop cool completely
  • Remove loose debris
  • Wipe surface with damp cloth
  • Identify burn mark types

Step 2: Apply Cleaning Solution

  • Make baking soda paste
  • Apply to burn marks
  • Let sit for 10 minutes
  • Spray with vinegar

Step 3: Gentle Scrubbing

  • Use non-abrasive scrubber
  • Work in circular motions
  • Avoid scratching surface
  • Repeat if necessary

Step 4: Final Touches

  • Clean with damp cloth
  • Dry thoroughly
  • Apply stovetop polish
  • Buff to shine

Common Problems

Stubborn Stains

Solution: Use commercial cleaner specifically for your stovetop type

Surface Scratches

Solution: Use only non-abrasive cleaners and soft cloths

Cooked-On Food

Solution: Let cleaning solution soak longer before scrubbing

Maintenance Tips

  • Clean spills immediately
  • Use appropriate cookware
  • Clean after each use
  • Avoid abrasive cleaners
  • Keep surface protected
