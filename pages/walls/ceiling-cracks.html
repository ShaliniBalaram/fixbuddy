Repair Ceiling Cracks

Learn how to fix unsightly ceiling cracks and prevent them from returning.

Tools & Materials

  • Joint compound
  • Drywall tape
  • Putty knife
  • Sandpaper
  • Paint and primer
  • Paint roller and brush
  • Drop cloths
  • Safety glasses and dust mask

Fix Steps

Step 1: Assess the Damage

  • Examine crack pattern
  • Check for water damage
  • Look for structural issues
  • Clean the area

Step 2: Prepare the Crack

  • Remove loose material
  • Widen crack slightly if needed
  • Clean dust away
  • Apply self-adhesive tape

Step 3: Apply Compound

  • Apply first layer thinly
  • Feather edges carefully
  • Let dry completely
  • Sand smooth

Step 4: Final Touches

  • Apply second coat if needed
  • Sand until smooth
  • Prime repaired area
  • Paint to match ceiling

Common Problems

Recurring Cracks

Solution: Use flexible caulk or investigate structural issues

Paint Matching

Solution: Paint larger section or entire ceiling

Texture Matching

Solution: Use spray texture or hire professional for matching

Maintenance Tips

  • Monitor for new cracks
  • Check attic ventilation
  • Control indoor humidity
  • Address water leaks promptly
  • Document crack patterns
