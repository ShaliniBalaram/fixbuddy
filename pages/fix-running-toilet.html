  1. Diagnose the problem:

  2. Check the flapper:
    • Inspect for wear
    • Clean flapper and seat
    • Adjust chain length
    • Replace if necessary
  3. Adjust water level:
    • Check float position
    • Adjust float arm/cup
    • Mark correct water level
    • Test flush cycle
  4. Replace fill valve:
    • Turn off water
    • Drain tank
    • Remove old valve
    • Install new valve
  5. Test and adjust:
    • Turn water on slowly
    • Check for leaks
    • Adjust water level
    • Test flush multiple times
