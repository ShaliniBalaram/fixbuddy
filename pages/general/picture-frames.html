Align Picture Frames Perfectly

Learn how to hang and align picture frames for a professional gallery wall look.

Tools & Materials

  • Level
  • Measuring tape
  • Pencil
  • Picture hanging hooks
  • Hammer
  • Wall anchors
  • Painter's tape
  • Laser level (optional)

Alignment Steps

Step 1: Plan Layout

  • Measure wall space
  • Arrange frames on floor
  • Take spacing measurements
  • Mark center points

Step 2: Mark Wall

  • Use level to draw lines
  • Mark hook positions
  • Verify measurements
  • Check spacing

Step 3: Install Hardware

  • Install wall anchors
  • Add picture hooks
  • Test hook stability
  • Clean marks

Step 4: Hang and Align

  • Hang center frame first
  • Check level
  • Add remaining frames
  • Make final adjustments

Common Problems

Uneven Spacing

Solution: Use template or measure carefully

Crooked Frames

Solution: Use level and adjust hanging wire

Wall Damage

Solution: Use appropriate anchors for wall type

Maintenance Tips

  • Check alignment monthly
  • Clean frames regularly
  • Secure loose hardware
  • Keep hanging tools
  • Document arrangement
