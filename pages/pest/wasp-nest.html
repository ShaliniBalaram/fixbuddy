Remove a Wasp Nest Safely

Learn how to safely remove wasp nests from your property while minimizing risks.

Safety Warning

Wasp removal can be extremely dangerous. If you have allergies to wasp stings, are dealing with a large nest, or the nest is in a hard-to-reach location, ALWAYS contact a professional pest control service. Never attempt removal if you're unsure about safety.
