Seal Cracks to Prevent Insects

Learn how to effectively seal entry points to keep insects out of your home.

Safety Warning

Use proper ventilation when working with sealants. Wear appropriate protective gear. Keep children and pets away from work areas and materials.

Common Issues

Failed Seals

Check for proper adhesion. Clean surface thoroughly. Use appropriate sealant type.

Recurring Gaps

Look for structural movement. Consider flexible sealants. Address underlying issues.

Hidden Entry Points

Use thorough inspection methods. Consider professional assessment. Monitor pest activity.
