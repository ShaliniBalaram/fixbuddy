üìù Step-by-Step Instructions

  1. Prepare for replacement:
    • Turn off light switch
    • Wait for bulb to cool
    • Position ladder safely if needed
  2. Remove old bulb:
    • Grip bulb base firmly
    • Turn counterclockwise
    • Support bulb while turning
  3. Check specifications:
    • Verify correct bulb type
    • Check wattage requirements
    • Note base type and size
  4. Install new bulb:
    • Hold by base, not glass
    • Insert and turn clockwise
    • Don't overtighten
  5. Test and finish:
    • Turn on switch to test
    • Dispose of old bulb properly
    • Clean up work area
üí° Energy Saving Tips:
  • Choose LED bulbs for longest life and lowest energy use
  • Match bulb brightness (lumens) not watts
  • Consider smart bulbs for automated control
  • Keep track of warranty information
