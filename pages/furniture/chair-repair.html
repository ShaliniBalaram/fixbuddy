How to Fix Common Chair Problems

Beginner 15-45 minutes

From wobbly chairs to loose legs, learn how to fix common chair problems and extend their lifespan.

Common Problems & Solutions

Wobbly Chair

Chair rocks or feels unstable when sitting.

Solution:

  1. Identify uneven leg
  2. Check all joints
  3. Tighten loose screws
  4. Add furniture pads
  5. Level chair legs

Loose Chair Leg

Chair leg is detached or loose from the seat.

Solution:

  1. Remove old glue
  2. Clean joint surfaces
  3. Apply wood glue
  4. Clamp firmly
  5. Allow to dry completely

Squeaky Joints

Chair makes noise when used.

Solution:

  1. Locate noise source
  2. Tighten all fasteners
  3. Apply wood glue to joints
  4. Add lubricant if needed
  5. Test chair stability

What You'll Need

Screwdrivers
Hammer
Wood glue
Clamps
Level

Maintenance Tips

Check joints monthly

Tighten screws regularly

Replace worn pads

Clean joints annually
