Wood Furniture Care Guide

Learn how to properly maintain and protect your wooden furniture to ensure its longevity and beauty.

Regular Maintenance

Daily Care

  • Dust with a soft, lint-free cloth
  • Clean spills immediately
  • Use coasters under drinks
  • Avoid direct sunlight exposure

Weekly Care

  • Clean with appropriate wood cleaner
  • Check for water marks
  • Rotate decorative items

Monthly Care

  • Apply furniture polish
  • Check humidity levels
  • Inspect for any issues

Best Practices

Temperature Control

Maintain consistent room temperature and humidity levels to prevent wood from warping or cracking.

Cleaning Products

Use only cleaning products specifically designed for your wood type. Avoid all-purpose cleaners.

Preventive Measures

Use felt pads under items, maintain proper humidity, and keep furniture away from heat sources.

Seasonal Maintenance

Spring

  • Deep clean with appropriate wood cleaner
  • Check for winter damage
  • Adjust humidity controls

Summer

  • Protect from direct sunlight
  • Monitor air conditioning effects
  • Check for humidity damage

Fall

  • Prepare for heating season
  • Apply protective coating
  • Check for summer sun damage

Winter

  • Monitor indoor humidity
  • Keep away from heating vents
  • Use humidifiers if needed
