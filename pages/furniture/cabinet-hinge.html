How to Fix Cabinet Hinges

Beginner Friendly 15-30 minutes

Cabinet hinges can become loose, squeaky, or misaligned over time. Learn how to fix common cabinet hinge problems and keep your cabinets working smoothly.

Common Problems & Solutions

Loose Hinges

Cabinet doors that sag or don't close properly often have loose hinge screws.

Solution:

  1. Support door weight
  2. Tighten all screws
  3. Replace stripped screws
  4. Use wood filler if needed
  5. Test door alignment

Squeaky Hinges

Dry or rusty hinges can cause annoying squeaks when opening cabinets.

Solution:

  1. Clean hinge thoroughly
  2. Remove any rust
  3. Apply lubricant
  4. Wipe excess
  5. Test movement

Misaligned Doors

Cabinet doors that don't line up properly can be adjusted using the hinge mechanisms.

Solution:

  1. Identify adjustment screws
  2. Adjust side-to-side
  3. Adjust up and down
  4. Adjust door depth
  5. Check alignment

What You'll Need

Screwdrivers
Lubricant
Wood filler
Level
Cleaning supplies

Maintenance Tips

Check and tighten hinge screws every 6 months

Clean and lubricate hinges annually

Avoid hanging heavy items on cabinet doors

Keep a record of hinge type for easy replacement
