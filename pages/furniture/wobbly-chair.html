Fix a Wobbly Chair

Learn how to stabilize and repair a wobbly chair safely and effectively.

Safety Warning

Ensure the chair is properly supported while working on it. Test the stability thoroughly before use to prevent accidents.

Tools & Materials

  • Wood glue
  • Screwdriver set
  • Wood shims
  • Level
  • Sandpaper (medium and fine grit)
  • Clean rags
  • Clamps (if needed)
  • Wood filler (optional)

Fix Steps

Step 1: Assess the Problem

  • Turn the chair upside down
  • Check all joints and connections
  • Identify loose or damaged parts
  • Test each leg for stability

Step 2: Tighten Hardware

  • Tighten all visible screws
  • Replace any stripped or damaged screws
  • Check for missing hardware

Step 3: Fix Loose Joints

  • Clean old glue from joints
  • Apply wood glue to loose joints
  • Clamp and let dry for 24 hours

Step 4: Level the Chair

  • Place chair on level surface
  • Add shims where needed
  • Trim excess shim material

Common Problems

Loose Screws

Solution: Replace with slightly larger screws or fill holes with wood filler before re-screwing

Split Wood

Solution: Apply wood glue, clamp, and allow to dry completely before use

Uneven Legs

Solution: Use shims or sand down longer legs to match

Maintenance Tips

  • Check chair stability monthly
  • Tighten screws and bolts regularly
  • Clean joints and connections
  • Avoid dragging chairs on floor
  • Keep chairs away from moisture
  • Address loose parts immediately
  • Use furniture pads on legs
  • Store spare hardware
