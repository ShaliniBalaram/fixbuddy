How to Fix Stuck Drawers

Beginner Friendly 15-30 minutes

Stuck drawers are a common furniture problem that can be frustrating. Learn how to diagnose and fix drawer issues to restore smooth operation.

Common Problems & Solutions

Drawer Track Issues

Debris or damage in drawer tracks can cause sticking and rough operation.

Solution:

  1. Remove drawer completely
  2. Clean tracks thoroughly
  3. Check for damage
  4. Apply lubricant
  5. Reinstall drawer

Swollen Wood

Humidity can cause wooden drawers to expand and stick.

Solution:

  1. Remove drawer if possible
  2. Sand swollen areas
  3. Apply wax to sides
  4. Consider dehumidifier
  5. Test movement

Broken Slides/Guides

Damaged or broken drawer slides can prevent proper operation.

Solution:

  1. Remove drawer
  2. Inspect slide mechanism
  3. Purchase replacement slides
  4. Install new hardware
  5. Align properly

What You'll Need

Screwdriver set
Lubricant
Cleaning supplies
Sandpaper
Measuring tape

Maintenance Tips

Clean drawer tracks regularly to prevent buildup

Apply lubricant to slides every 6 months

Don't overload drawers beyond their capacity

Control room humidity to prevent wood swelling
