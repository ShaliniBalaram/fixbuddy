Fix Squeaky Floorboards

Eliminate annoying floor squeaks and creaks

üí° Pro Tip:

The best time to fix squeaky floors is during dry weather when wood is naturally contracted.

  • Screwdriver
  • Hammer
  • Shims
  • Talcum powder or powdered graphite
  • Drill (optional)
  • Specialized squeak-relief brackets (optional)

Estimated Time: 30-60 minutes

üìù Step-by-Step Instructions

  1. Locate the squeak:
    • Walk on the floor to find exact spots
    • Mark squeaky areas with tape
    • Determine if accessible from below
  2. For accessible floors (with basement/crawl space):
    • Have someone walk above while you observe
    • Look for gaps between joists and subfloor
    • Insert shims where needed
  3. For floors without access below:
    • Locate floor joists
    • Drive screws through finish floor into joists
    • Countersink screws carefully
  4. Quick fixes:
    • Sprinkle talcum powder between boards
    • Work powder into gaps with a brush
    • Walk on area to work in powder
  5. Test and adjust:
    • Walk on repaired areas
    • Add more shims if needed
    • Clean up any excess powder
