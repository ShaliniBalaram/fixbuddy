Fix Hardwood Floor Scratches

Learn how to repair scratches in hardwood flooring and restore its beauty.

Tools & Materials

  • Wood filler or putty
  • Stain marker or touch-up pen
  • Fine-grit sandpaper
  • Wood stain
  • Polyurethane finish
  • Clean cloths
  • Paint brush
  • Safety glasses and mask

Fix Steps

Step 1: Assess the Damage

  • Clean the scratched area
  • Determine scratch depth
  • Match wood color
  • Choose repair method

Step 2: Light Scratches

  • Use stain marker
  • Apply in direction of grain
  • Wipe excess immediately
  • Let dry completely

Step 3: Deep Scratches

  • Fill with wood putty
  • Sand smooth when dry
  • Apply matching stain
  • Allow to dry fully

Step 4: Finish and Protect

  • Apply polyurethane
  • Match existing finish sheen
  • Allow proper cure time
  • Blend edges carefully

Common Problems

Color Matching

Solution: Test stain on inconspicuous area first

Visible Repairs

Solution: Blend repair into surrounding area gradually

Finish Differences

Solution: Match sheen level of existing floor finish

Maintenance Tips

  • Use furniture pads
  • Clean floors regularly
  • Address scratches promptly
  • Maintain proper humidity
  • Avoid dragging furniture
