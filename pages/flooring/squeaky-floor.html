Fix a Squeaky Floorboard

Learn how to eliminate annoying floor squeaks and creaks with these proven methods.

Tools & Materials

  • Drill
  • Screwdriver
  • Carpenter's square
  • Shims
  • Wood screws
  • Talcum powder or powdered graphite
  • Drill bits
  • Safety glasses

Fix Steps

Step 1: Locate the Squeak

  • Walk on floor to find squeaky spots
  • Mark problem areas
  • Identify floor joist locations
  • Check for gaps between subfloor and joists

Step 2: Try Simple Solutions First

  • Apply talcum powder between boards
  • Work powder into gaps
  • Test floor movement
  • Repeat if necessary

Step 3: Secure Loose Boards

  • Drill pilot holes
  • Insert screws into joists
  • Countersink screws
  • Fill screw holes if needed

Step 4: Add Support

  • Insert shims in gaps
  • Secure shims with adhesive
  • Trim excess material
  • Test floor stability

Common Problems

Seasonal Changes

Solution: Use flexible solutions like shims that can accommodate wood movement

Multiple Squeaks

Solution: Address each area individually, working from worst to least

Hidden Joists

Solution: Use a stud finder to locate joists before drilling

Maintenance Tips

  • Maintain consistent humidity levels
  • Use rugs in high-traffic areas
  • Check for loose boards regularly
  • Address squeaks when they first appear
  • Keep floor clean and dry
