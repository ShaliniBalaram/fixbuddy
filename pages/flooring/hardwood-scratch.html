Patch Scratched Hardwood Floors

Learn how to repair scratches in hardwood flooring and restore its beauty.

Safety Warning

Use proper ventilation when working with stains and finishes. Wear appropriate protective equipment. Keep work area well-ventilated. Some products are flammable - keep away from heat sources.

Tools & Materials

  • Wood filler
  • Putty knife
  • Sandpaper (various grits)
  • Wood stain markers
  • Wood finish
  • Clean cloths
  • Protective gloves
  • Face mask

Repair Steps

Assess Damage

  • Clean area thoroughly
  • Determine scratch depth
  • Note wood color
  • Check finish type

Surface Scratches

  • Use wood marker
  • Apply carefully
  • Blend edges
  • Let dry completely

Deep Scratches

  • Clean scratch thoroughly
  • Apply wood filler
  • Sand smooth
  • Match color

Finish Application

  • Apply matching finish
  • Blend with surrounding area
  • Allow proper drying
  • Buff if needed

Prevention Tips

  • Use furniture pads
  • Place rugs in high-traffic areas
  • Clean floors regularly
  • Remove shoes indoors
  • Trim pet nails
  • Avoid dragging furniture
  • Regular maintenance
  • Quick response to damage

Common Issues

Color Matching

Test stain on inconspicuous area first. Mix colors if needed for perfect match.

Finish Blending

Feather edges of repair area for seamless transition to existing finish.

Multiple Scratches

Consider refinishing entire board or section for consistent appearance.
