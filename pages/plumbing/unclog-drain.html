Unclog a Sink or Shower Drain

Learn how to effectively clear clogged drains using safe and proven methods.

Tools & Materials

  • Plunger
  • Drain snake or auger
  • Baking soda
  • White vinegar
  • Boiling water
  • Drain cleaning solution (optional)
  • Rubber gloves
  • Bucket

Fix Steps

Step 1: Try Boiling Water

  • Remove standing water if possible
  • Pour boiling water directly into drain
  • Wait 5 minutes
  • Test if drain is flowing

Step 2: Use a Plunger

  • Create seal around drain
  • Plunge vigorously 5-10 times
  • Run water to test flow

Step 3: Natural Cleaning Solution

  • Pour 1/2 cup baking soda down drain
  • Follow with 1/2 cup vinegar
  • Cover drain immediately
  • Wait 15 minutes
  • Flush with hot water

Step 4: Use a Drain Snake

  • Insert snake into drain
  • Turn handle clockwise
  • Pull out debris
  • Flush with hot water

Common Problems

Recurring Clogs

Solution: Install a drain strainer and regularly clean it to prevent debris buildup

Chemical Damage

Solution: Avoid harsh chemical cleaners; use natural solutions or mechanical methods instead

Slow Drainage

Solution: Regular maintenance cleaning with baking soda and vinegar monthly

Maintenance Tips

  • Use drain strainers in all drains
  • Flush drains weekly with hot water
  • Clean pop-up stoppers monthly
  • Avoid pouring grease down drains
  • Schedule professional cleaning annually
