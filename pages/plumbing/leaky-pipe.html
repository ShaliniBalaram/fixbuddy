How to Fix Leaky Pipes

Intermediate 30-60 minutes

Leaky pipes can cause water damage and increase your water bill. Learn how to identify and fix common pipe leaks safely.

For major leaks or pipe damage, always consult a professional plumber!

Common Problems & Solutions

Joint Leaks

Water leaking from pipe joints or connections is a common issue.

Solution:

  1. Shut off water supply
  2. Dry the area completely
  3. Tighten connection fittings
  4. Apply pipe tape if needed
  5. Check for continued leaks

Pipe Cracks

Small cracks or pinhole leaks in pipes.

Solution:

  1. Turn off water
  2. Clean pipe surface
  3. Apply epoxy putty
  4. Allow to cure
  5. Test repair

Frozen Pipe Damage

Pipes that have burst or cracked due to freezing.

Solution:

  1. Shut off main water valve
  2. Open faucets to drain
  3. Replace damaged section
  4. Add pipe insulation
  5. Test new pipe section

What You'll Need

Pipe wrench
Pipe tape
Epoxy putty
Measuring tape
Cleaning supplies

Maintenance Tips

Regularly inspect pipes for signs of corrosion

Insulate pipes in cold areas

Monitor water pressure regularly

Know location of main water shut-off valve
