Basic Pipe Repairs

Learn how to handle common pipe issues, from minor leaks to basic replacements.

Safety Warning

Always turn off main water supply before working on pipes. Use proper safety equipment. For major repairs, burst pipes, or sewage issues, contact a licensed plumber immediately. Be cautious of water damage and mold.

Tools & Materials

  • Pipe wrench
  • Adjustable wrench
  • Pipe cutter
  • Plumber's tape
  • Repair clamps
  • Replacement pipes
  • Safety gloves
  • Bucket and towels

Common Problems & Solutions

Small Leaks

  • Locate exact leak point
  • Clean pipe surface
  • Apply repair tape/clamp
  • Monitor for effectiveness

Joint Problems

  • Remove old joint
  • Clean connecting surfaces
  • Apply new sealant
  • Reassemble properly

Pipe Replacement

  • Measure carefully
  • Cut pipe cleanly
  • Prepare connections
  • Install new section

Frozen Pipes

  • Locate frozen section
  • Apply gentle heat
  • Check for damage
  • Prevent future freezing

Prevention Tips

  • Insulate exposed pipes
  • Monitor water pressure
  • Check for corrosion
  • Fix small leaks promptly
  • Know pipe locations
  • Prevent freezing
  • Regular inspections
  • Keep repair kit ready
