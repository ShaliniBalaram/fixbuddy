Sink and Basin Repairs

Learn how to fix common sink problems, from leaks to slow drains and damaged fixtures.

Safety Warning

Turn off water supply before starting any repairs. Use caution with tools in wet areas. Avoid chemical drain cleaners. For major plumbing issues, consult a professional plumber.

Tools & Materials

  • Basin wrench
  • Adjustable wrench
  • Plumber's tape
  • Plunger
  • Drain snake
  • Plumber's putty
  • Safety gloves
  • Bucket

Common Problems & Solutions

Leaky Faucet

  • Identify leak source
  • Replace washers/O-rings
  • Check valve seats
  • Replace cartridge if needed

Slow Drain

  • Remove and clean stopper
  • Use plunger properly
  • Snake the drain
  • Clean P-trap

Loose Fixtures

  • Tighten mounting nuts
  • Replace mounting hardware
  • Apply plumber's putty
  • Secure faucet base

Low Water Pressure

  • Clean aerator
  • Check shut-off valves
  • Inspect supply lines
  • Clear mineral deposits

Prevention Tips

  • Clean drains regularly
  • Use drain strainers
  • Check for leaks monthly
  • Clean aerators periodically
  • Avoid harsh chemicals
  • Don't overtighten fittings
  • Address issues promptly
  • Keep tools accessible
