Water Heater Maintenance

Keep your water heater running efficiently with proper maintenance and troubleshooting.

Safety Warning

Hot water can cause severe burns. Turn off power/gas and allow unit to cool before maintenance. For gas heaters, be alert for gas leaks. For major repairs or gas issues, contact a licensed professional immediately.

Tools & Materials

  • Garden hose
  • Bucket
  • Adjustable wrench
  • Screwdriver set
  • Voltage tester
  • Safety gloves
  • Safety glasses
  • Anode rod (if needed)

Maintenance Tasks

Annual Flush

  • Turn off power/gas
  • Connect drain hose
  • Drain tank completely
  • Flush until clear

Anode Rod Check

  • Locate rod
  • Remove and inspect
  • Replace if needed
  • Seal properly

Prevention Tips

  • Annual maintenance check
  • Monitor water quality
  • Check pressure regularly
  • Insulate pipes
  • Listen for unusual sounds
  • Keep area clean
  • Document maintenance
  • Know shutoff locations
