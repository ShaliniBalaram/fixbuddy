How to Fix Low Water Pressure

Intermediate 30-90 minutes

Low water pressure can affect your daily activities. Learn how to diagnose and fix common water pressure problems.

If you suspect a main line issue, contact your water utility company!

Common Problems & Solutions

Clogged Aerator

Mineral buildup in faucet aerator reducing flow.

Solution:

  1. Remove aerator
  2. Disassemble parts
  3. Soak in vinegar
  4. Clean with brush
  5. Reassemble and test

Pressure Regulator

Faulty pressure reducing valve.

Solution:

  1. Locate regulator
  2. Check pressure gauge
  3. Adjust settings
  4. Test pressure
  5. Replace if needed

Pipe Buildup

Mineral deposits in pipes restricting flow.

Solution:

  1. Identify affected pipes
  2. Flush system
  3. Use descaling solution
  4. Run water thoroughly
  5. Monitor pressure

What You'll Need

Adjustable wrench
Pressure gauge
Cleaning brush
Vinegar
Pliers

Maintenance Tips

Clean aerators monthly

Check pressure regularly

Flush pipes annually

Monitor water quality
