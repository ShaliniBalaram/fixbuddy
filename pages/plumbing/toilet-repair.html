Common Toilet Problems

Learn how to diagnose and fix common toilet issues, from running toilets to weak flushes.

Safety Warning

Turn off water supply before starting repairs. Avoid using harsh chemicals. Keep work area clean and sanitized. For sewage backups or major clogs, contact a professional plumber.

Tools & Materials

  • Plunger
  • Toilet auger
  • Adjustable wrench
  • Replacement parts (as needed)
  • Rubber gloves
  • Bucket
  • Cleaning supplies
  • Towels

Common Problems & Solutions

Running Toilet

  • Check flapper for proper seal
  • Adjust or replace chain
  • Clean rim jets
  • Replace fill valve if needed

Weak Flush

  • Clear rim jets
  • Check water level
  • Inspect flush valve
  • Clean siphon jet

Clogged Toilet

  • Use plunger properly
  • Try toilet auger
  • Remove and clean trap
  • Check vent stack

Leaking Base

  • Check wax ring
  • Inspect floor flange
  • Tighten mounting bolts
  • Replace seal if needed

Prevention Tips

  • Don't flush non-degradable items
  • Clean bowl regularly
  • Check for leaks monthly
  • Maintain proper water level
  • Replace parts before they fail
  • Use proper cleaning products
  • Avoid chemical drain cleaners
  • Keep plunger nearby
