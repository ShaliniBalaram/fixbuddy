Plumbing Maintenance Guide

Regular maintenance of your home's plumbing system helps prevent issues and extends the life of your fixtures.

Safety Warning

Always turn off water supply before working on plumbing. Be cautious with chemicals and tools. If you encounter serious issues like burst pipes or sewage backups, contact a licensed plumber immediately. Keep electrical devices away from water.

Tools & Materials

  • Pipe wrench
  • Plunger
  • Drain snake
  • Plumber's tape
  • Pipe cleaning brushes
  • Safety gloves
  • Flashlight
  • Bucket

Maintenance Schedule

Weekly Tasks

  • Run water in unused fixtures
  • Check for visible leaks
  • Listen for running toilets
  • Clean shower heads

Monthly Tasks

  • Test water pressure
  • Clean drain strainers
  • Check caulking
  • Inspect exposed pipes

Quarterly Tasks

  • Clean water heater
  • Test shut-off valves
  • Check for pipe corrosion
  • Inspect outdoor plumbing

Annual Tasks

  • Professional inspection
  • Water heater flush
  • Pipe insulation check
  • Septic system service

Prevention Tips

  • Know main water shut-off location
  • Never pour grease down drains
  • Use drain strainers
  • Fix leaks promptly
  • Maintain proper water pressure
  • Protect pipes from freezing
  • Schedule regular inspections
  • Keep records of maintenance
