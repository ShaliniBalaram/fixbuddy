Plumbing Maintenance Guide

Regular maintenance of your home's plumbing system helps prevent issues and extends the life of your fixtures.

Regular Maintenance Schedule

Weekly Tasks

  • Run water in unused sinks and showers
  • Check for visible leaks under sinks
  • Listen for running toilets
  • Clean shower heads and faucet aerators

Monthly Tasks

  • Test water pressure in all fixtures
  • Clean all drain stoppers and strainers
  • Check caulking around fixtures
  • Inspect exposed pipes for condensation

Seasonal Tasks

  • Inspect all visible pipes for signs of wear
  • Clean and maintain water heater
  • Check outdoor spigots and hoses
  • Insulate pipes in unheated areas

Preventive Measures

Water Pressure Management

Maintain optimal water pressure between 40-80 PSI to prevent strain on pipes and fixtures.

Drain Care

Use drain strainers in all sinks and tubs. Flush drains monthly with hot water.

Water Quality

Test water quality annually and maintain water treatment systems as recommended.

Seasonal Maintenance

Spring

  • Check for winter pipe damage
  • Clean outdoor drainage systems
  • Test sump pump operation

Summer

  • Inspect irrigation systems
  • Check outdoor faucet connections
  • Monitor basement humidity

Fall

  • Prepare pipes for winter
  • Drain outdoor water features
  • Check water heater settings

Winter

  • Insulate exposed pipes
  • Keep indoor temperature consistent
  • Know location of main water valve

Water Conservation Tips

  • Install low-flow fixtures
  • Monitor water bills for unusual changes
  • Fix drips and leaks promptly
  • Consider water-efficient appliances
