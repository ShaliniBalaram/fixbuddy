How to Fix a Cracked Tile

  • Replacement tile
  • Tile adhesive
  • Grout
  • Grout sealer
  • Chisel and hammer
  • Safety goggles
  • Work gloves
  • Dust mask
  • Putty knife
  • Sponge and bucket
  1. Put on safety gear (goggles, gloves, and dust mask)
  2. Remove the damaged tile carefully using a chisel and hammer
  3. Clean the exposed area thoroughly
  4. Apply new tile adhesive to the back of the replacement tile
  5. Place the new tile in position
  6. Allow adhesive to dry completely (follow manufacturer's instructions)
  7. Apply grout around the tile
  8. Clean excess grout with a damp sponge
  9. Allow grout to dry
  10. Apply grout sealer

  • Save a few extra tiles when installing new flooring for future repairs
  • Use spacers to ensure even spacing around the replacement tile
  • Mix only small amounts of grout at a time
  • Consider the room's ventilation when working with adhesives
