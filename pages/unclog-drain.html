üìù Step-by-Step Instructions

  1. Try boiling water:
    • Clear standing water
    • Pour boiling water slowly
    • Wait 5 minutes
    • Test drain flow
  2. Use a plunger:
    • Cover overflow hole
    • Add water to create seal
    • Plunge vigorously
    • Test drain flow
  3. Try natural solution:
    • Pour baking soda
    • Add vinegar
    • Cover drain
    • Flush with hot water
  4. Use drain snake:
    • Insert snake into drain
    • Turn handle clockwise
    • Pull out debris
    • Flush with hot water
  5. Clean and maintain:
    • Remove drain cover
    • Clean visible debris
    • Install drain strainer
    • Run hot water

  • Use drain strainers
  • Don't pour grease down drains
  • Run hot water regularly
  • Clean drains monthly
