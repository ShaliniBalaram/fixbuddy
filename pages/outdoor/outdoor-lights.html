Replace Cracked Outdoor Light Fixtures

Learn how to safely replace damaged outdoor light fixtures while maintaining proper electrical safety.

Safety Warning

Always turn off power at the circuit breaker before working on electrical fixtures. Use proper safety equipment. If unsure, consult a licensed electrician.

Common Issues

No Power

Check breaker. Verify connections. Test switch. Inspect wiring.

Water Damage

Improve sealing. Check drainage. Update fixture. Add coverage.

Flickering

Check bulb. Inspect connections. Clean contacts. Verify voltage.
