üéØ Common Stains Guide

  • Coffee/Tea: Blot with vinegar solution
  • Red Wine: Club soda or salt
  • Pet Accidents: Enzymatic cleaner
  • Mud: Let dry, vacuum, then clean
  • Ink: Rubbing alcohol

üìù Step-by-Step Instructions

  1. Act quickly:
    • Blot up liquid spills immediately
    • Scrape up solid material
    • Never rub the stain
  2. Prepare cleaning solution:
    • Mix appropriate solution for stain type
    • Use warm water when needed
    • Keep solutions mild to protect carpet fibers
  3. Clean the stain:
    • Work from outside toward center
    • Blot with clean white cloth
    • Apply cleaning solution sparingly
  4. Rinse and dry:
    • Blot with clean water
    • Remove all cleaning solution
    • Use fans to speed drying
  5. Final treatment:
    • Vacuum when completely dry
    • Check for remaining stain
    • Repeat if necessary
üí° Pro Tip:

For stubborn stains, place a clean white cloth dampened with cleaning solution over the stain, then place a heavy book on top for several hours. This helps the solution penetrate deeper into the carpet fibers.
