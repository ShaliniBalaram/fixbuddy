Restore and protect your tile grout lines

  • Utility knife or grout saw

Estimated Time: 2-3 hours (plus 24-48 hours curing time)

üí° Pro Tip:

Take a sample of your existing grout to the store to ensure a perfect color match. Grout color can vary significantly between brands and batches.

üìù Step-by-Step Instructions

  1. Prepare the area:
    • Clean tiles thoroughly
    • Remove loose or damaged grout
    • Vacuum debris from joints
  2. Mix the grout:
    • Follow manufacturer's instructions
    • Mix small batches as needed
    • Achieve toothpaste-like consistency
  3. Apply new grout:
    • Hold float at 45-degree angle
    • Press grout into joints
    • Work in small sections
    • Remove excess with float
  4. Clean tiles:
    • Wait 15-30 minutes
    • Wipe with damp sponge
    • Rinse sponge frequently
    • Remove haze with clean cloth
  5. Seal the grout:
    • Wait 48-72 hours for complete curing
    • Apply sealer according to instructions
    • Apply second coat if recommended
‚ö†Ô∏è Maintenance Tip:

Reseal your grout every 1-2 years to prevent staining and water damage. Regular sealing extends the life of your grout significantly.
