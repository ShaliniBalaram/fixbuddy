Safety Precautions

Before starting any repairs:

  • Unplug the washer from the electrical outlet
  • Turn off both hot and cold water supplies
  • Keep work area dry and well-ventilated
  • Wear appropriate safety gear

Fixing Drainage Issues

Common Causes

  • Kinked or clogged drain hose
  • Blocked pump filter
  • Faulty drain pump
  • Improper drain hose installation

Solution Steps

  1. Check and straighten any kinks in the drain hose
  2. Locate and clean the drain pump filter
  3. Verify drain hose height (should be 24-96 inches)
  4. Test drain pump operation
  5. Clear any blockages in the drain system
