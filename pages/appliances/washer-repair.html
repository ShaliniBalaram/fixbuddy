How to Fix Common Washer Problems

Intermediate 30-90 minutes

Washing machine problems can disrupt your daily routine. Learn how to diagnose and fix common washer issues safely.

Always unplug the washer before attempting any repairs!

Common Problems & Solutions

Washer Won't Drain

Standing water in the washer after a cycle is often due to drainage issues.

Solution:

  1. Check drain hose for kinks
  2. Clean drain pump filter
  3. Remove drain hose blockage
  4. Verify pump operation
  5. Test drain function

Excessive Vibration

Unbalanced loads or improper leveling can cause violent shaking.

Solution:

  1. Level the washer
  2. Check shock absorbers
  3. Inspect suspension springs
  4. Balance the load
  5. Test spin cycle

Not Filling with Water

When the washer isn't getting enough or any water.

Solution:

  1. Check water supply valves
  2. Clean inlet screens
  3. Inspect fill hoses
  4. Test water inlet valve
  5. Verify water pressure

What You'll Need

Cleaning supplies

Maintenance Tips

Clean the drain pump filter monthly

Check hoses for wear every 6 months

Clean the drum regularly

Leave door open between uses to prevent mold
