Stove & Oven Maintenance Guide

Maintenance Schedule

Daily Care

  • Wipe spills immediately

    No cleaning spray? Mix equal parts vinegar and water

  • Clean cooktop after use

    Use baking soda paste for tough stains

  • Check burner function

    Uneven flame? Clean burner ports with a safety pin

Weekly

  • Deep clean burners
  • Clean oven if heavily used
  • Check door seal

Monthly

  • Deep clean oven
  • Check gas connections
  • Test all functions

Common Issues & Quick Fixes

Burner Won't Light

  1. Clean the burner head and ports
  2. Check for food debris blocking gas flow
  3. Ensure proper burner alignment

Household Tools You Can Use:

  • Safety pin or toothpick for cleaning ports
  • Old toothbrush for scrubbing
  • Vinegar solution for dissolving grease

Pro Maintenance Tips

Prevent Scratches

Use appropriate cookware and avoid sliding pots and pans across the surface.

Timing Matters

Clean spills immediately to prevent baked-on residue.

Temperature Control

Use appropriate heat settings to prevent damage and ensure efficiency.
