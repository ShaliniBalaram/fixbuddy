Clean the Filter in an AC Unit

Learn how to properly clean and maintain your air conditioner's filter to ensure optimal performance and air quality.

Safety Warning

Always turn off and unplug the AC unit before cleaning. Wear a dust mask when cleaning filters. Be careful with water around electrical components.
