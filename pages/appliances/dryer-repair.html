How to Fix Common Dryer Problems

Intermediate 30-90 minutes

A malfunctioning dryer can leave you with wet clothes and frustration. Learn how to diagnose and fix common dryer issues safely.

Always unplug the dryer and shut off gas (if applicable) before attempting repairs!

Common Problems & Solutions

Not Heating

When the dryer tumbles but clothes aren't getting dry.

Solution:

  1. Check power/gas supply
  2. Clean lint filter thoroughly
  3. Clear vent ductwork
  4. Test heating element
  5. Check thermal fuse

Noisy Operation

Unusual sounds during operation often indicate mechanical issues.

Solution:

  1. Check drum support rollers
  2. Inspect belt condition
  3. Test idler pulley
  4. Verify drum alignment
  5. Replace worn parts

Won't Start

When the dryer shows no signs of power or operation.

Solution:

  1. Verify power connection
  2. Check door switch
  3. Test start switch
  4. Inspect thermal fuse
  5. Check drive motor

Maintenance Tips

Clean lint filter after every load

Clean vent ductwork annually

Check vent hose for damage regularly

Don't overload the dryer
