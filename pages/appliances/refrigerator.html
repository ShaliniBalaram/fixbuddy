Replace a Refrigerator Door Seal

Learn how to replace a damaged or worn refrigerator door seal (gasket) to improve energy efficiency and maintain proper temperature.

Safety Warning

Ensure the refrigerator is unplugged before starting any repair work. Handle sharp edges with care. Keep track of all screws and parts.
