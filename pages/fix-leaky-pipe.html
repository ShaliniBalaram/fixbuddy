  1. Locate and assess leak:
    • Find exact leak location
    • Determine leak type
    • Measure pipe diameter
    • Clean and dry area
  2. Prepare the area:
    • Turn off water
    • Drain pipes
    • Clean pipe surface
    • Place bucket under leak
  3. Temporary fix (if needed):
    • Apply epoxy putty
    • Install repair clamp
    • Wrap with tape
    • Monitor leak
  4. Permanent repair:
    • Cut out damaged section
    • Prepare replacement piece
    • Apply joint compound
    • Connect new section
  5. Test and finish:
    • Turn water on slowly
    • Check for leaks
    • Tighten if needed
    • Clean work area
