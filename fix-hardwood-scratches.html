Fix Hardwood Floor Scratches

Restore your hardwood floors to their original beauty

  • Surface Scratches: Only affect the finish
  • Minor Scratches: Slightly penetrate the wood
  • Deep Scratches: Significantly penetrate the wood
  • Gouges: Deep indentations requiring wood filler

  • Wood repair markers or crayons
  • Wood putty or filler
  • Fine-grit sandpaper (220 grit)
  • Steel wool (#0000)
  • Clean cloths
  • Wood stain (matching color)
  • Polyurethane finish
  • Small brush or applicator

Estimated Time: 1-2 hours (plus drying time)

‚ö†Ô∏è Important:

Test any repair products on an inconspicuous area first. Some floors may require professional refinishing for the best results.

üìù Step-by-Step Instructions

  1. Clean the area:
    • Remove dirt and debris
    • Clean with wood floor cleaner
    • Let dry completely
  2. For surface scratches:
    • Use repair marker/crayon
    • Apply in direction of grain
    • Wipe excess with cloth
    • Buff with steel wool
  3. For deeper scratches:
    • Sand area lightly
    • Clean dust thoroughly
    • Apply wood filler
    • Let dry completely
    • Sand smooth
  4. Color matching:
    • Apply matching stain
    • Blend with surrounding area
    • Allow to dry
  5. Finishing:
    • Apply polyurethane finish
    • Use thin, even coats
    • Allow proper drying time
    • Buff if needed
üí° Pro Tips:
  • Use walnuts to naturally fix light scratches - the oils help hide the damage
  • Keep touch-up markers in your floor's color for quick fixes
  • Consider using felt pads under furniture to prevent future scratches
